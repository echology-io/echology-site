What is in the package

1

Signal Audit

Full corpus harvest and deterministic classification across your entire document environment. Gap detection as the primary output: what should be there and isn't.

2

Signal Build

The three-twin deliverable: True Twin (how the business actually runs), Optimized Twin (how it should run), and the Delta Report with prioritized interventions.

3

Metadata Organizational Map

Who edits what, how often, with whom. Knowledge-concentration risk surfaced by deterministic analysis of edit frequency, authorship, and review participation.

4

Role Twins (top 5 personnel)

For each of your five most senior people: a structural model of the work they do, built from their actual document output. Not a questionnaire. The evidence itself.

5

Org Map

The relationships between the Role Twins. Who depends on whom, which decisions route through which person, where the single-point-of-failure risks live.

6

Geode Citation Audit

A Surface (Geode) pass over your published expertise: what AI systems currently cite when asked about your domain, what they should be citing, and the citation-ready artifacts that close the gap.

7

Signal Operate (6 months included)

Six months of continuous harvest, gap detection, and review-gate management. You keep the system; we keep it sharp while your team learns to run it.
