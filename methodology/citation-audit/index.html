AECai / aecai.io

Result: 2/5 queries show brand presence.

Query Found Top Results
"AI for AEC industry" No aiinaec.com, Trimble, OpenAsset, aec+tech
"construction document intelligence" No Autodesk, AWS, Document Crunch (2x), Mantis Builder
"AI for engineering documents" No Flowcase, Colab Software, generic listicles

"aecai" Yes (#4) aecai.es (Spanish beer assoc.) holds #1-3

Key finding: Branded term contested by aecai.es. Direct competitors: Document Crunch, BuildPulse AI, Mantis Builder, Sonar Labs. AECai has strongest baseline of the three brands.

Cross-Brand Findings

Zero third-party mentions for any brand. AI systems cite content that other sites reference. Wikipedia, listicles, comparison articles drive AI citations. None of the three brands appear in these contexts.

Geode has the most open field. The GEO space is new. Competitors are positioning but no one dominates yet. First Page Sage is the loudest but has not locked the space.

echology faces the hardest positioning challenge. "Document intelligence" is Azure's term. Need to find different query targets that are not owned by hyperscalers.

AECai has the most tractable path. Niche market, moderate competition, already indexing for some queries. Targeted answer pages could move from #10 to top 3.

All three need answer pages, backlinks, and structured content. The sites exist but have zero external authority signals.
