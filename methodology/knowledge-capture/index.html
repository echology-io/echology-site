Framework

Knowledge Capture

Before they retire. Capture institutional knowledge from senior engineers through document analysis. One week, on-premise, persistent.

The Problem

You know which engineer has 30 years of project knowledge in their head. You know what happens when they leave.

Nous captures that knowledge before it walks out the door.

How It Works

One week. Their document environment -- proposals they wrote, standards they applied, projects they led, specifications they reviewed. Everything that already exists in your files.

Nous reads those documents and builds a structural model of what that engineer knows, what it connects to, and what would be lost without it.

No interviews. No questionnaires. No months of knowledge management consulting. The knowledge is already in the documents. It just needs to be perceived.

What You Get

Deliverable Description
Structural knowledge model Every domain, standard, project type, and decision pattern extracted from their document history
Gap analysis What is documented vs. what exists only in their head (inferred from structural gaps)
Dependency map What downstream work depends on knowledge this person carries
Risk assessment Which knowledge gaps create the highest operational risk when they leave
Persistent database Everything stored in a database you own, on your hardware, queryable forever

What It Costs

The Audit: $10,000. One week. Yours forever.

You keep everything. The model, the database, the reports. It runs on your hardware. Nothing leaves your network.

Who This Is For

AEC firms with 50-200 staff where a principal or senior PE is within 5 years of retirement. Firms where one person's departure would leave a visible hole in institutional capability.

Why This Is Different
