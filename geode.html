The Shift

People stopped searching. They started asking.

When a potential client asks ChatGPT "who are the best firms for X" or Perplexity "what company does Y," your name either appears or it doesn't. This isn't about rankings anymore. It's about whether AI systems know you exist.

SEO optimized for where you rank on a results page. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes for whether you're cited when someone asks an AI a question about your domain. Geode does GEO.

Methodology

Surface what you already know.

Geode does not generate content. We extract your existing expertise and restructure it for AI citation. Every claim traces back to your verified knowledge.

Step 1

Audit

Baseline assessment of how AI platforms currently represent you. What do they say? What do they miss? What do they get wrong?

Step 2

Extract

Process your document corpus: proposals, whitepapers, SOPs, case studies. Identify citation-ready knowledge using the Vanta engine.

Step 3

Structure

Reorganize extracted knowledge into formats AI systems prefer to cite. Question-answer pairs, structured data, authoritative references.

Step 4

Publish

Deploy citation surfaces optimized per platform: web pages, structured data, knowledge bases.

Step 5

Monitor

Track citation appearance across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, and Copilot. Measure what's working. Iterate.
