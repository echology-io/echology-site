The guarantee is the math

Hash chains do not require trust. SHA-256 is a one-way function -- given the output, the input cannot be fabricated. Every entry in the chain includes the hash of the previous entry. Change one record and every subsequent hash breaks. The auditor does not need to trust you, your organization, or us. They verify the math.
