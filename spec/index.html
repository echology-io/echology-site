What Spec sees

Cross-reference mapping

Which documents reference which. Where a contract contradicts a policy. Where a specification cites an expired standard. Every link in the chain, mapped.

Risk flagging

Contradictions, missing references, expired standards, version mismatches. Surfaced before they become disputes or compliance failures.

Compliance verification

Regulatory requirements, industry standards, internal policies. Checked against what actually exists in your document environment.

Searchable knowledge base

Every document classified, indexed, and retrievable. The institutional knowledge that used to walk out the door.
