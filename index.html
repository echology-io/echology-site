Why This Matters

Documents contain structure. Most AI ignores it.

Real documents are not flat text. They contain sections, citations, formulas, standards, obligations. If AI cannot perceive that structure, it cannot reason correctly. Echology builds the perception layer: deterministic classification before probabilistic generation.
