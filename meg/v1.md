# MEG MVP Architecture - v1 (Baseline)

10 sections. First pass. Broad strokes of the platform with enough technical detail to show the shape.

## Sections

1. **Platform Overview** - P2P energy trading platform, 4 pillars (blockchain, AI/ML, IoT, matching engine), regulatory reality callout
2. **System Architecture** - ASCII diagram: Prosumer Dashboard > FastAPI > Redis/Postgres/TimescaleDB/EWC + MQTT broker > Edge nodes
3. **Technology Stack** - Table: EWC Solidity, FastAPI, PostgreSQL+TimescaleDB, Redis, MQTT, XGBoost/LSTM/Prophet, RPi4+SDM630, Next.js, Docker Compose
4. **Core Components** - Smart contracts (ParticipantRegistry, EnergyToken ERC-1155, EnergyMarket, Settlement), on-chain vs off-chain split, chain config (Volta/EWC/Besu), IoT pipeline, matching engine (periodic double auction + continuous bilateral), AI/ML overview (forecasting, pricing, anomaly detection)
5. **Build Plan** - 4 phases: Core Settlement (M1-2), Intelligence Layer (M3-4), Edge + Hardware (M5), Dashboard + Launch (M6)
6. **Infrastructure Cost** - 6-month MVP estimate (~$340-540/mo managed services)
7. **Regulatory Strategy** - 4 tiers: behind-the-meter, community solar, utility partnership, wholesale (FERC 2222)
8. **Competitive Landscape** - Power Ledger, LO3/Pando, WePower, Electrify, GridPlus
9. **Technical Risks** - 5 risks with probability/impact/mitigation
10. **What Makes This Defensible** - Network effects, regulatory moat, data moat, vertical integration, portability
