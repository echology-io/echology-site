How we measured

Every step in the initiative system has an effort field (estimated) and an actual_effort field (recorded at completion). The command ops effort-report generates the comparison from the database. No manual estimation after the fact.

What comes next

This is deployment 001: echology on itself. The next deployment will be a client's business. Same methodology. Same measurement. If the numbers are different, we will report them honestly. The system that measures its own efficiency cannot hide from its own data.
