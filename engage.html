Four steps.
One week.

You provide document access. The system does the rest.

1

You grant document access.

Shared drives, file servers, cloud storage. Whatever your team uses. The system needs to see what exists. Read-only access is sufficient.

2

The system scans and classifies.

Every document is indexed, typed, and mapped. Contracts, SOPs, reports, correspondence, proposals. The system identifies what each file is and how it connects to others.

3

Three models score independently.

Each AI system analyzes the same data separately. Consensus findings carry high confidence. Divergent findings are flagged for review. This eliminates single-model bias.

You receive the Signal Report.

Structural findings, operational model, and scored analysis. Every output traceable to its source. Your team reviews it. No one needs to sell you on the next step -- the findings speak.
