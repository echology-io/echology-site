Data Type Retention Period
Database backups 30 days rolling

Health check data 7 days

Deletion Procedures

When you request deletion, we follow a documented procedure:

  1. Identify all data associated with your engagement
  2. Delete source documents from local filesystem
  3. Delete all database records for the engagement
  4. Delete vector embeddings
  5. Purge from rolling backups within 30 days
  6. Provide written confirmation of deletion

Live data deletion: within 5 business days. Backup purge: within 30 days.

Data Location

No data is stored in cloud services, third-party databases, or external storage systems. All processing and storage occurs on hardware under your control or ours.

For data deletion requests or retention questions: hello@echology.io
